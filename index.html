What We Do

10 Automations. One Platform. Zero Extra Staff.

Every service My Smart Slots offers is included below. Each one runs automatically once we set it up for your business.

💬

AI Chat Booking

A smart chatbot lives on your customer's website and answers questions, captures lead info, and books appointments directly — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, with no staff required.

📲

Missed Call Text Back

The moment a call goes unanswered, your customer automatically receives a text with a booking link. Most people will book right there — before they even think about calling someone else.

✅

SMS Confirmations

Every time a job gets booked, an automatic SMS goes out to confirm the appointment — date, time, and details. Your customer is locked in and your crew shows up to a real job.

🔔

Appointment Reminders

Automated reminders go out before every appointment — 24 hours ahead and again the morning of. No-shows drop dramatically. Your schedule becomes predictable again.

📱

Live SMS Chat

Customers can text your business number and get instant intelligent responses — scheduling questions, pricing info, availability — handled automatically in real time.

⭐

Post-Job Follow-Up

After every completed job, an automatic message goes out to the customer asking how it went and requesting a review. More 5-star reviews. More trust. More inbound calls.

🔄

Lead Re-Engagement

Old leads, cold inquiries, and past customers who haven't booked recently get a professional automated re-engagement sequence — bringing them back to your calendar without you lifting a finger.

📧

Partial Email Replies

When a customer emails or submits a web form, an instant automated reply goes back acknowledging their inquiry and directing them toward booking — keeping them warm while you're on the job.

📅

Calendar Sync

We connect directly to your existing Google or Outlook calendar, or set up a free Calendly for you. Every booking lands exactly where it needs to — no double-booking, no manual entry.

📊

Monthly Report

Every month you receive a clear report showing calls recovered, jobs booked, confirmations sent, reviews generated, and estimated revenue impact — straight numbers, no fluff.
